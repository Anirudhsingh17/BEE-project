Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do wireless headphones connect to devices? +
Wireless headphones connect via Bluetooth, allowing easy pairing with smartphones, tablets, and laptops.
What should I consider when buying a soundbar? +
Look for sound quality, connectivity options, size, and compatibility with your TV or devices.
Do higher watt speakers always mean better sound? +
Not necessarily. Wattage affects volume, but sound quality depends on design, drivers, and acoustics.
How do I improve the bass on my headphones? +
Adjust the equalizer settings on your device or choose headphones with built-in bass enhancement features.
